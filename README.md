Smart Tiffin & Student Analytics Platform

Smart Tiffin & Student Analytics Platform is a modern full-stack SaaS application designed for both individual students and tiffin service owners.

Students can track personal expenses, manage budgets, monitor meal consumption, and gain insights into their spending habits through advanced analytics.

Tiffin service owners can manage customers, track daily meal attendance, generate monthly bills, monitor payments, and analyze business performance using interactive dashboards and reports.

The platform supports independent usage for both user types while also providing an optional connection system that allows students to join a tiffin service and automatically sync meal attendance, billing, and payment information.
